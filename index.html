What We Do

Endurance, or aerobic, activieties increase your breathing and heart rate. They keep your heart, lungs, and circulatory system healthy and improve your overall fitness. Examples include brisk walking, jogging, swimming, and biking.
Strength, or resistance traning, exercise make your muscles stronger. Some examples are lifting weights and using a resistance band.
Balance exercise can make it easier to walk on uneven surfaces and prevent falls. To improve your balance, try tai chi or exercises like standing on one leg.
Flexibility exercise stretch your muscles and can help your body stay limba. Yoga and doing variuos stretches can make you more flexible.
